#  Key Insights

##  Challenges
- Low-performing pizzas impacting revenue
- Sales drop in certain months (Oct, Sep)
- Very low demand for XL sizes

---

##  Opportunities
- Promote best-selling pizzas
- Introduce combo offers
- Increase marketing in low-performing months

---

##  Recommendations
- Focus on high-demand categories (Classic & Large size)
- Remove or redesign low-performing products
- Optimize pricing strategy
- Improve weekend promotions
